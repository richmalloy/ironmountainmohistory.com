The underground workings of the Main and N.W. orebodies, stacked at their true relative depths beneath the plant. Levels are named in feet below the shaft collar, per the Ozark Ore / Hanna map series (1944–1966 era).
⚠ Interpretive model: level outlines are stylized from the state repository's preview scans (Maps 00222–00234, 00636–00681) and Murphy 1961 — not survey-traced. Depth-below-collar reading of level names is a working assumption (the 803 Level matches the remembered ~800 ft of hoist rope). To be superseded by a georeferenced model from high-resolution scans.
