THE 691 LEVEL

Six hundred and ninety-one feet below the hoist house collar. Mined 1944–1966; abandoned with the pumps off; flooded ever since. You are walking it as it was on a working day, by cap lamp — the pillars holding the roof are the mountain's own rock, left standing by the engineers.

Interpretive reconstruction from Map 00224 and the Hanna series — pillar layout stylized until the high-resolution scans are traced.
